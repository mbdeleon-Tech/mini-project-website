What are the Sustainable Development Goals?

The Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) are a universal call to action to end poverty, protect the planet, and ensure that by 2030 all people enjoy peace and prosperity. Our initiative focuses on three key areas where we can make the most impact for Filipino communities across the archipelago.

Our Focus Areas in the Philippines

💙

Good Health & Well-being

Improving healthcare access and services for Filipino communities, providing medical assistance and health education across the archipelago.

Learn More →
📖

Quality Education

Empowering Filipino children and adults through education programs that build skills and create opportunities for a brighter future.

💼

Decent Work & Economic Growth

Creating livelihood opportunities and promoting economic empowerment through skills training, entrepreneurship, and job placement.
